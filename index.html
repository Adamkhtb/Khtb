Our Philosophy

Art isn’t extra, it’s essential.

We believe creative expression is a fundamental part of being human. Our programs are not about making perfect art. They are about creating moments of presence, connection, and self discovery. Every session is designed with care, led with warmth, and shaped around the people in the room.

Meet the Founder

The heart behind Hearts Creative—designing each experience with care, warmth, and intention.

Nadia El-Khatib

Creative Director and Art Educator

Nadia El-Khatib is a visual arts educator and creative visionary with over 20 years of experience, specializing in art therapy and using creativity as a pathway to healing and connection. Through her creative arts company, she designs meaningful experiences that help people of all ages connect with their creativity and discover the joy of making.
Follow her work on Instagram at @in_my_art_classroom.
