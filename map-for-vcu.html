About

MAP4VCU is a collaborative project created in a human-centered design course by classmates Inho Park, Chelsee Sahonero, and myself.

We wanted to solve the issue of trip planning for new students on the VCU campus and sought to do so by creating a more accessible source of information.

Understanding that there was no reason to reinvent already existing services over the course of a single semester it was decided the best course of action would to be provide already existing site for a bike paths, bike parking, and car parking maps.

The component designed by our team was the "RVA Transit Options" Tab.
