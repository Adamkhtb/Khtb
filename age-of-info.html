Age of Info

About

We exist in an era where information is extremely easy to access. While this can be a good thing, the information we encounter in our daily lives can be easily manipulated or entirely fabricated. Therefore, it is important that people are aware of strategies to avoid misinformation.

However, with the creation of digital media, the constant bombardment of information can become difficult to navigate.

This satirical publication emphasizes the danger of the absolute faith people have in information provided by authorities, media outlets, or social media. It also graphically displays the black hole of never-ending information we encounter in our lives.
