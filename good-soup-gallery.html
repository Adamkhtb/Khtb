About

After being prompted to create a publication involving a topic that mattered to me I chose the topic of “good soup”. This publication follows a mundane and soup-obsessed narrator who is attempting to fill his sorrows with the temporary pleasures provided by a can of soup. The pages are filled with drawn-out and boring descriptions of various soups. However, we see the narrator grow tired of his coping habits through the imagery shown. He is on the verge of a breakthrough in his cycle of soup but ultimately falls short in his self-reflection
